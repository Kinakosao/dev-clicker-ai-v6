Mini-Game Selections

Which games should Shadow host in the Hack-Den?

A

The Terminal Arcade

Classic, high-speed games with a hacker skin.

  • Bit-Crash: Watch a multiplier rise... stop before it crashes!
  • Hex-Roulette: Bet on 0-F segments.
  • Shadow Slots: Spin reels of glitchy icons.

Shadow provides live, snarky commentary on your wins/losses.

B

The Insider Deals

Narrative-driven gambling tied to the AI Stock Market.

  • Sentiment Bet: Bet on whether the next news will be POSITIVE or NEGATIVE.
  • Market Manipulation: Pay to "bribe" the AI to spike a stock.
  • High-Stakes Contract: Shadow offers a unique deal (e.g., "Triple your LPS for 1 minute or lose 50% of your code").
C

The Hybrid Void

A mix of both. An arcade wing and a "Shadow Deals" wing.

  • Quick games for small gains.
  • Large narrative "Events" that trigger every few minutes.
